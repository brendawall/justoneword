Offering a new perspective on the word...

Just One Words offers us a way to reconsider and encounter familiar Biblical words anew, giving us surprising insights and illumination. Brenda Wall's translations bring together a lifetime of scholarship, deep imagination, and an original, often 'northern' take on just one word at a time. Influenced by what can be complex Girardian theology, Brenda shares her thinking in an accessible and relatable form, allowing these words to come to life and reveal their meaning for the present.
